About Us

We blend ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with cutting-edge blockchain technology to create a transparent, authentic, and sustainable traceability system — from soil to shelf.

Our Mission

To empower farmers, wild collectors, and Ayurvedic manufacturers by providing a blockchain-based platform that ensures every herb is authentic, sustainable, and geo-tagged from origin to formulation.

Our Vision

A world where every Ayurvedic herb carries a digital story of purity, sustainability, and trust — strengthening Ayurveda’s global credibility.
